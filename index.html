Welcome to Grace of Jesus Christ Ministries

A prophetic church where lives are transformed by the Word, the Spirit, and the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ.

The Word is Alive

"Look now, there is in this city a man of God, and he is an honorable man; all that he saith cometh surely to pass: now let us go thither; peradventure he can shew us our way that we should go." — 1 Samuel 9:6 (KJV)

A Place of Encounter

Through prophetic utterance, anointed worship, and Spirit-led teaching, we invite you to encounter the Holy Spirit — healing, restoring, and igniting purpose in every heart.

Jesus Christ is Exalted

The Word is alive, the Spirit moves, and Jesus Christ is exalted. Join us in worship every Sunday at 8:00 AM for powerful praise and life-changing teaching.
